## Leaderboard impact

No new public leaderboard row was added in this pass.  The useful public-facing
updates are interpretive:

- the high-agreement compiler package makes the already-promoted tangent
  threshold gate easier to audit and reuse;
- the `{2,3}`-smooth count is Paper B theory infrastructure, not a finite
  prize-row numerator;

  high-agreement finite-threshold gate, so it is retained as a finite witness
  audit rather than added as a best row;
